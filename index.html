Services



Waxing

A cloth or paper strip is applied and pressed firmly, adhering the strip to the wax and the wax to the skin. The strip is then quickly ripped against the direction of hair growth, as parallel as possible to the skin to avoid trauma to the skin

Manicure

A cosmetic treatment of the hands involving cutting, shaping, and often painting of the nails, removal of the cuticles, and softening of the skin.

Pedicure

A pedicure is a cosmetic treatment for the feet and toenails. Similar to a manicure but for the feet, a pedicure includes a foot soak, callus removal, a short foot and leg massage, a toenail trim, application of cuticle oil and toenail polish.

Lashes & Brows

Services include lash and brow tints, lash extensions, lash lifts, lash removal and more. Most of our lashes & brows treatments enable you to literally throw away your makeup!
